The Project

We have developed a sensor that will gather information about your plants' well-being.
By co-operating with Twitter you will be able to receive a status report from your plant, including temperature and soil moisture.

The Company

Smart Garden consists of three students from the Information Technology programme at Nösnäsgymnasiet. We work with an organisation called UF, or, Youth Enterprise to make this company a possibility. Our school, Nösnäsgymnasiet, is a part of TeknikCollege.

UF

UF is an organisation made to teach students about enterprising. The students get to brainstorm ideas and inventions, these are later used to start up companies. During this enterprise course, the students get to attend exhibitions and sales to promote their company and gather new ideas.

Our School

Nösnäsgymnasiet is a highschool located in Stenungsund, Sweden. It is part of Teknikcollege. This means that the students are provided with skills that are attractive in the job market. Teknikcollege provides very high-quality education.
